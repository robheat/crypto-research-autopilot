Solana's token recovery (+4.3%) is the largest in the watchlist, and PENGU riding alongside suggests that Solana's on-chain activity is the preferred venue for speculative capital returning during bounces.

Whether this is organic community hype or coordinated engineering, its durability as a top-7 trending name during a broad market recovery warrants attention. The market is clearly assigning some probability to a mainnet catalyst or exchange listing event.

The privacy narrative (ZEC) that dominated trending during the selloff has been fully extinguished from the top seven. One-day durability, not structural.
